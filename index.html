openBarter

openBarter is a database schema defining primitives of a barter market. It is also an extension of the postgreSQL database.

It is an attempt to invent an alternative to the financial system supervising human economy, since archaic barter has always been the result of a crumbling financial system. openBarter that allow multilateral exchanges should be nearly as liquid as a regular market using money.

It is impossible for a B747 pilot to land by using only a brake and a gas pedal. Likewise, money is mono-dimensional and cannot be the single command to restore ecologic stability of the planet. By considering value as multi-dimensional, openBarter proposes an allocation mean of a diversity of scarce ecological resources.

It implements the economic mechanisms of a regular market and allows cyclic exchange between more than two partners (buyer and seller) in a single transaction. Even if competition is performed between bids (exchange proposals) , it does not require any central monetary standard to quantify the value exchanged and express prices to be compared.

The database finds possible relations between bids and submit draft agreements for approval by owners. When agreements are accepted by partners, the property of related values is exchanged between partners.

Mathematic principles of openbarter are detailed on this paper.
