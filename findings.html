Experiments

Results presented were obtained to observe variations of foor indicators for different size of the order book:

  • the mean execution time of an order (delay),
  • the mean number of movements per cycle (nbcycle),
  • fluidity of the market that is a measurement of it's capacity to drain values out of the order book (fluidity),
  • the ratio between omega produced by movements and minimum omega required by the corresponding order (gain).


Results presented here were produced by version 7.0.1 of openBarter.

Experimentation protocol

The order book is filled with barter limit orders where the ratio qtt_prov/qtt_requ between the quantity provided and the quantity required is chosen randomly using a continuous uniform distribution between 0.5 and 1.5, and where the quantity provided (qtt_prov) is 10 000. The order book is filled by producing first a big book with the desired statistic, then extracting the beginning of the book to obtain the chosen size. For this given size, results presented are the means of values obtained by submitting 1000 barter limit orders. This set of orders submitted is the same for all size.

The fluidity of the market is defined as the ratio between the output flow of values produced by movements and the input flow of values offered by orders submitted to the order book. Flows of value are obtained by summing quantities of values for all qualities. The input flow is 10 000 (qtt_prov) * 1000 orders, and the output flow is the sum of quantities moved.

regular and barter market compared

For a scenario money we consider a special quality used by all orders, as on a regular order book where a sell order asks for € and a buy order offers €. The quality that is not € is chosen randomly with a uniform distribution between 99 other qualities.

A second scenario uni100 is made of barter orders where the diversity of quality is 100 with a uniform distribution.

These measurements leads the following comments:

  • For a given volume of the order book, the fluidity of the market is 30% better with uni100 than with the money scenario.
  • A higher delay with uni100 means the computation is more expensive.
  • The high gain of money is due to higher competition, and high nbcycle of uni100 is due to higher cooperation.
  • The number of cycles greater than 2 of the money scenario means that some cycles produced can have more than 2 partners. There are cycles where four partners A,B,C,D exchange qualities Q1,Q2,€ where A provides Q1 to B, B provides € to C, C provides Q2 to D and D provides € to A. This cycle should be better for partners than two separate bilateral cycles or the single exchange possibility.

big order book

These measurements leads the following comments:

  • The variation of response time is low (between 15 and 18 ms) and independent of the volume.
  • For the same flow of value brought by orders, the output flow is reduces by the gain increase.
  • nbcycle is reduced as the volume grows. The reasons could be that the limits of exploration can be reached before cycles with more partners can be explored.

sensibility of limits

Constants are defined by the model to limit computation load :

  • The maximum number of movement per cycle (Y)
  • The maximum number of movement per transaction (M)
  • The maximum number of cycle explored (P)

Different limits were used:

  • E1: Y=6 P=1024 M=128
  • E2: Y=6 P=2048 M=128
  • E3: Y=12 P=1024 M=128
  • E4: Y=6 P=1024 M=256
A diversity of qualities 100 was used to show the sensibility of these limits.

These measurements leads the following comments:

  • The execution time is sensible to P but not significantly dependant on other factors.
  • Fluidity is slightly improved by P, but is independant of other limits.
  • The number of movements per cycle is usually less than 6. Larger Y allow more exchanges with a small order book.
  • The gain is always better than 1 due to the order type (LIMIT). The gain grows as the size of the book due to competition increase. Small order books are not representative of a statistical population.

This experiment could be also performed by changing the maximum number of partners per cycle.

diversity of qualities

Different diversity of qualities are considered (10,100 and 1000).

These measurements leads the following comments:

  • The fluidity of the market increases when de diversity decreases.
  • Greater gain reveals higher competition for low diversity of qualities.
  • The number of partners in cycles increses when the diversity grows.

This experiment could be also performed by changing the maximum number of partners per cycle to find out it's influence on the curves.
