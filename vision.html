Elements of architecture

Decentralized

If openBarter is organized as a central limit order book, a depository (green) can use several markets (red). The centrality of the order book means that competition occurs on all orders it contains, but the web of exchange is decentralized.

Depository

A common trusted digital depository stores the representation of values (quality and quantity) with the name of the owner, that is a depository. When values are exchanged, names of owners are just swapped. It is not physical values that change hands.

A quality is a measurement standard represented by a name of the form depositoryName/qualityName.

A quantity is an integer.

A couple (quality,quantity) can represent any fungible value such as mineral, money, or Joule.

An owner can record a value on a depository that accept this quality.

Relation between market and depositories

The schema represents the back office on the left, and the front office on the right.

The market executes orders entered by owners. The movements produced are then executed to the depositories.

A common authentication such as oAuth is used to authenticate users. They act as owners on the depositories and on the market. This way, a user can receive a value even if he is not yet known as an owner by the depository managing this value.

Market users have access to the history of past transactions using statistics prepared with a stat engine providing a technical analysis of the market extended to multilateral exchanges.
